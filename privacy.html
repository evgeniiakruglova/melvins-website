Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

G.O.A.L. Training Academy ("we", "us", "our") operates the website goaltrainingacademy.vercel.app and manages messaging on Instagram and Facebook for Coach Melvin Agyei-Boateng's soccer training services in Lakeland, Florida.

Information We Collect

When you contact us through our website, Instagram, or Facebook, we may collect:

  • Contact information: your name, email address, and phone number (if you provide them)
  • Message content: the messages you send us through our website contact form, Instagram DMs, or Facebook Messenger
  • Child information: your child's name, age, and soccer experience (if you share this with us to help us recommend training)
  • Platform data: your public profile name from Instagram or Facebook when you message us

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries about our training programs
  • Recommend appropriate training options for your child
  • Schedule training sessions
  • Send you follow-up information about our services
  • Improve our services and communication

Automated Messaging

We use an automated messaging system to provide quick initial responses when you message us on Instagram or Facebook. This system helps us respond promptly to common questions about our programs, pricing, and availability. Coach Melvin personally handles all scheduling and booking conversations.

Data Storage

Your information is stored securely in our private business management system (Google Workspace). We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only accessible to Coach Melvin and authorized team members.

Data Retention

We retain your contact information and message history for as long as needed to provide our services to you. If you would like us to delete your data, please contact us using the information below.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you

  • Request deletion of your data
