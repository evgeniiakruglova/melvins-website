About the Coach

Meet Coach Melvin Agyei-Boateng

Ghana U-20 National Team
Tampa Bay Rowdies
Florida Tropics FC
Saint Leo University

From the streets of Kumasi, Ghana to professional stadiums in the United States, Coach Melvin's journey in soccer has been defined by discipline, passion, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

A former professional player who represented Ghana's U-20 National Team in 2012 and went on to play for the Tampa Bay Rowdies and Florida Tropics FC, Coach Melvin brings real-world experience to every training session.

After an accomplished playing career that included competing at Saint Leo University and in professional leagues across the U.S., Coach Melvin founded Goal Training Academy in Lakeland, Florida with one mission: to develop the next generation of elite soccer players through personalized, high-intensity training.
